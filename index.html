Project 02
Long-Term Care Setting
2020 — 2021

Virtual Program Launch and
COVID-19 Services Manual

Crisis Response Remote Program Design Technical Writing Stakeholder Communication Inclusive Design Collaborative Delivery
The Problem
Pandemic isolation with zero remote infrastructure.

A sudden complete lockdown left residents with no programming, no family connection, and no structure for their days. There was no existing playbook for virtual delivery in a long-term care setting and no resources for families trying to support engagement from outside the facility.

My Approach
Designed a virtual program and co-authored a standardized manual.

Collaborated with a two-person team to design and launch a virtual programming model, applying evidence-based practices adapted across diverse cognitive and physical ability levels. Co-developed the COVID-19 Recreation Services Manual, distributed to staff and resident families.

The Outcome
20% wellbeing improvement. A reusable, scalable playbook.

Measured a 20% improvement in participants' reported overall wellbeing during mandatory isolation. The standardized manual became a reusable resource beyond the original scope, distributable to any long-term care facility navigating similar circumstances.
